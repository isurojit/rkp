Professional Profile

CA Ravi Kumar Patwa

Managing Partner at RKP Associates, one of Eastern India's most distinguished Chartered Accountancy firms. A leader in the profession with extensive experience across multiple sectors and a proven track record of excellence in audit, taxation, and consultancy services.

Academic Excellence

  • Secured 6th rank in All India and 2nd in Eastern India in the DISA (Diploma in Information System Audit) Examination conducted by ICAI
  • Completed extensive professional training in auditing, taxation, and corporate laws
  • Continuous professional development through national and international programs

Professional Activities

  • Conducted Training Sessions for officials of Controller & Auditor General of India
  • Trained Managing Directors, Directors & General Managers of Public Sector Undertakings including BRPL and Financial Institutions like NEDFI
  • Regular speaker at national and international CA conferences and seminars across India
  • Represented India at two Indo-Bangladesh Trade Summits
  • Delivered lectures in Universities, Colleges and Educational Institutions on academic, economics & related matters
  • Member of Governing Bodies of Educational and Social Service Organizations

Committee Memberships & Contributions

  • Official Reviewer of the Guidance Note on Audit of State Co-operative Banks & District Co-operative Banks published jointly by NABARD & ICAI
  • Member of Study Group to review Auditing and Assurance Standards issued by ICAI in light of New Companies Act, 2013
  • Member of group to 'Study and Suggest Changes in Guidelines relating to Networking of CA Firms' formed in 2016
  • Member of Reserve Bank of India's State Level Co-ordination Committee for NBFCs & Un-incorporated bodies accepting public deposits (2008-09)
  • Member of Young Members' Skill & Innovation Development Committee of ICAI (2018-19)
  • Member of Direct Taxes Committee (DTC) of ICAI (2017-18)
  • Member of Professional Development Committee (PDC) of ICAI (2016-17)
  • Member of Committee for Capacity Building of CA Firms and Small & Medium Practitioners (CCBCAF & SMP) of ICAI (2012-13)

Leadership Achievements

  • First person from outside branch headquarter city to be Chairman of any Branch of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India
  • Under his Chairmanship, for the first time any branch from Eastern India earned National Awards
  • Guwahati Branch won 2 National Awards under his Chairmanship
  • Received Best Branch in Eastern India Award
  • Nominated for Vikash Ratna Gold Award by the International Integration & Growth Society, New Delhi (2002)

Social Activities

  • Actively involved in various social welfare activities
  • Member of Governing Bodies of Educational Institutions
  • President of Government Boys Higher Secondary School, an educational institution established in 1863
  • Trustee & Managing Committee Member of Hindustan Medical Institution (Birla Group)
  • Held various important positions in Social Service Organizations
